Privacy Policy - Streamoji

Last updated: 2025-01-01

This Privacy Policy applies to Streamoji (“the App”), which allows users to convert stickers from compatible websites into WhatsApp stickers.

Data Collection

The App does not collect any personally identifiable information from users.

The App processes image files that the user selects for the purpose of generating stickers. These files remain on the user’s device unless the user manually shares them via other apps.

Use of Data

  • No personal data is collected or stored.
  • No analytics are used.
  • No logs are stored.
  • No user or device data is shared with any third party.

Third Party Content

The App may include links to external sticker/image sources. We do not control external websites.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not target children under 13.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page.
